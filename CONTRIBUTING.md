# Contributing to pdf2md

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## Legal requirements

By submitting any contribution to this repository, you confirm that:

- You have read and agree to the terms in [CLA.md](./CLA.md).
- Your contribution is compatible with **AGPL-3.0-or-later** and with the CLA terms.
- You have the legal right to submit the contribution.

## Testing expectations

- Routine tests should remain lightweight and should **not require heavy OCR/model dependencies**.
- Please add or update lightweight tests for your changes where possible.
- If a change depends on optional heavy components, keep those paths optional and document how they are validated.
